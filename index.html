Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about my repair services and guarantees.

Will I lose my data during the repair?

For standard repairs like batteries and screens, your data is 100% safe. For more complex issues (like bootloops), I always prioritize data retention, though it's highly recommended to back up your device if it is currently functional.

What kind of parts do you use?

I offer both high-quality aftermarket and premium OEM-grade parts to fit your budget. I also use genuine original pulls directly from donor devices to ensure factory-level performance.

How long does a typical repair take?

Most battery and screen replacements take only 15 to 25 minutes. Backglass repairs and housing swaps generally take around 30 minutes to 2.5 hours.

Do I need an appointment?

Yes, I operate strictly by appointment only. Please text, call, or email to get a free quote and schedule your drop-off time.
